Mission:

To improve the lives of children living in poverty by providing access to education, healthcare, and empowering the incapable and needy people of society by providing them rickshaws, sewing machines etc so that they could survive in this fragile life with respect.

Founded: 2019

Location: Bangladesh

Projects:

Pather Phul:

To fulfill the ignored desires of roadside children by delivering light of education to enlighten their lives. So that they wouldn't be called as Street kids(Tokai's) anymore, rather called as The Flowers of The Street( Pather Phul).

Disaster:

Supporting the homeless & endangered people who has lost their roof during disasters.

Ichheghuri:

Accomplishing the untold wishes of unprivileged people like rickshaw pullers,day labourers and beggers to make them smile.

Blood Donation:

To manage blood for patients during operations.

Soft Skills:

We encourage youngsters among us to work on developing the soft skills like leadership, communication, management etc, who are the future of the country.
