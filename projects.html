Here is a short description of the works of SWAPNOCHAYA :~ 1.Project Swapnochaya~ Empowering the incapable and needy people of society by providing them rickshaws, sewing machines etc so that they could survive in this fragile life with respect. 2.Project *_Pather Phul_* *(The Flower of the Streets)*~ To fulfill the ignored desires of roadside children by delivering light of education to enlighten their lives. So that they wouldn't be called as Street kids(Tokai's) anymore, rather called as The Flowers of The Street( _Pather Phul_). 3.Project Disaster Supporting the homeless & endangered people who has lost their roof during disasters. 4- project *ICHHEGHURI* Accomplishing the untold wishes of unprivileged people like rickshaw pullers,day labourers and beggers to make them smile. 5- Blood donation To manage blood for patients during operations. 6.Soft skills We encourage youngsters among us to work on developing the soft skills like leadership, communication, management etc, who are the future of the country.
